About

Welcome to my blog! My name is Jere Seilo and as of January 2023, I'm a first-year software engineering student at LUT University. I'm excited to share my first project with you: a blog built with Django, the popular Python web framework.

After completing an online course on Udemy that taught me the basics of Django, I'm eager to apply my new skills to real-world projects. This blog is my first attempt at building something from scratch using Django before moving forward to learn Django REST framework.

I would also like to highlight that I'm a noob in front-end development. I focused mainly on the back-end and "django stuff" and I didn't use any front-end frameworks for this project. However, I still used a bit of time to make the blog responsive and user-friendly. By no means is it perfect but I will keep learning new stuff to make my future projects even better! I'm always open to any feedback on how to improve my projects.
